About

I develop machine learning methods for single-cell sequencing data (scRNA-seq and scATAC-seq), with published work spanning variational autoencoders, neural ordinary differential equations, reinforcement learning, graph neural networks, and hyperbolic geometry applied to cell fate analysis and representation learning.
